What We Do

Detailing, Engineered.

Every service is a layer of protection and finish — from deep paint correction to molecular-level coatings that keep your car looking new for years.

01

PPF — Paint Protection Film

Invisible, self-healing film that shields your paint from stone chips, scratches and UV damage — keeping that factory finish intact for the long haul.

02

Steam Cleaning

High-temperature steam lifts grime, sanitises seats and carpets, and clears stubborn odours — a deep clean without harsh chemicals.

03

Foam Wash

A thick blanket of pH-balanced foam lifts dirt safely off the surface, then a careful rinse leaves your car spotless without a single swirl mark.

04

Ceramic Coating

A long-lasting nano-ceramic layer that bonds to your paint — boosting gloss, repelling water and dirt, and protecting against fading for years.

05

Polishing

Multi-stage machine polishing removes swirl marks and oxidation, restoring true depth and clarity to your paint before any protective coating.

06

4×4 & SUV Specialists

From everyday hatchbacks to heavy-duty SUVs and off-roaders — our bays and equipment are built for vehicles of every size.
